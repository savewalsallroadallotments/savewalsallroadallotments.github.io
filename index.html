IMPORTANT

Our beautiful allotments are under threat from the 2022 Commonwealth Games.
We are situated next to the Alexander Stadium where the athletic events will be held and Birmingham City Council wants to take our allotments and use the land for a 'queuing system' for the Games.
Please join our Facebook Group, follow Robert The Allotment Cat on Twitter and sign our petition

What's Happening?

We are under threat of closure because the 2022 Commonwealth Games are taking place in the same park where our allotments are situated. Since the beggining of february we have been running Change.org petition to save our allotments. We have been in contact with multiple news organisations such as ITV and Birmingham Mail.
Currently our petition has over 8,000 signitures, however we need to reach 10,000 - meaning the Council must debate it at their Scrutiny Committee
